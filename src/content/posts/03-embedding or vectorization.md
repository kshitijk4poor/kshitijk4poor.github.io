 It's like taking each word or a token and encoding it into a multidimensional space where similar words are located closer to each other. So, instead of just having individual words, we now have these numerical representations (vectors) that capture more information about the words and how they relate to each other.

The process of creating embeddings from tokens is often called "tokenization" or "vectorization". There are various ways to create embeddings, but one common approach is to use a pre-trained model that has been trained on a large corpus of text data. The model learns to associate each token with a vector in the embedding space, such that similar tokens are located close to each other in the space.
